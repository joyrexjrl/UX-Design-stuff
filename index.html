Scuba

Scuba Guide

Meet our premier scuba expert, Dr. Hastoophers D Ghoti. With 4 years (almost a lifetime for a fish) of scuba experience and extensive knowledge of the area, divers will be in good fins with Hastoophers as the guide.

Scuba gear can be rented for $200 - $250 depending on availability. Tanks are rented out for $130 for a pair of tanks.

Scuba certifications can be acquired for $500 and is accepted worldwide.

Shark Friends

Our shark friend’s love being around people and the beaches! Never fear though as our local sharks have all been trained, they are aware that humans are friends, not food. Some have even turned to veganism and only eat seaweed.

Orange Fin Dancers

Taniti proudly presents our very own Orange Fin Dancers! While in the land of scuba diving, there is a good chance the dancers will come by and give you a show of a life time (assuming the shark friends are not around). Trained in synchronized dancing seemingly from birth, the dancers are sure to mesmerize you with wiggles from the sea.
